## ⚙️ System requirements

Your computer needs a few things to run this tool.

* Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* Microsoft .NET Desktop Runtime 8.0 or newer.
* A stable internet connection.
* At least 4GB of RAM.

## 🔑 Linking your security data

You must connect your Microsoft 365 account to the dashboard to see your data.

1. Launch the application.
2. The software opens a login window.
3. Sign in with your Microsoft 365 administrative credentials. 
4. The app asks for permission to read your security logs.
5. Accept these permissions.
6. The dashboard begins to load your security data.

This process takes a few minutes. The dashboard gathers information from these sources:

* **Microsoft Defender XDR:** Monitors your devices for threats.
* **Entra ID Protection:** Tracks login activity and risks.
* **Intune:** Displays the status of your managed devices.
* **Compliance Center:** Shows your current safety policies.

## 📊 Using the dashboard

The main screen shows a summary of your security health. Use the tabs on the left to move between sections.

* **Dashboard:** See alerts and device health at a glance.
* **Devices:** Review the status of your computers and mobile devices.
* **Users:** Monitor account activity to spot suspicious logins.
* **Policies:** Check that your security rules work as intended.
* **Alerts:** View a list of recent security events.

Click any alert to see more details. The app highlights urgent items in red. Yellow marks items that need your attention soon. Green items indicate that your security settings follow best practices.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting common issues

If the dashboard does not show data, try these steps.

* **Check your internet connection.** The app needs a connection to pull live data.
* **Restart the application.** Close the app and open it again.
* **Re-authenticate.** Log out and sign back in to refresh your connection.
* **Update your permissions.** Ensure your Microsoft 365 account has a global administrator or security reader role. This role allows the app to view the data it needs.
* **Check for updates.** Open the GitHub release page to see if a newer version is available. Better versions fix known bugs and improve stability.

## 🛠️ Security and privacy

The software runs on your computer. It does not send your data to third-party servers. Your information stays within your Microsoft 365 environment. The application acts as a viewer only. It reads information but does not make changes to your settings unless you click specific buttons within the interface.
